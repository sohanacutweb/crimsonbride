    25+ Minimal Mehndi Designs That'll Charm Every Bride's Aesthetics

    Comments (0)Tuesday 13th August, 2019
    • Facebook
    • Twitter
    • Pinterest

    Divya Arora

    An old school at heart with subtle but intense hints of modern glam, she's always happily making the best of both worlds. With reading, Zentangling, and spilling her thoughts on paper, she keeps her creative streak always on point. Hogging food and dozing off often, comes naturally to her!

    Follow her on Instagram @divya.arora14

    More Less

    While mehndi makes up for an imperative part of an aesthetic bridal look and brides love getting their hands and feet etched with henna, we all have come across the ones who’re not a huge fan of this custom. Also, this is an era of modern and unconventional brides breaking the cliched stereotypes whilst setting their own trends. Which is why decking up sans mehndi at their wedding or choosing a lighter design is totally acceptable.

    Now, you know how much we love spoiling all you to-be-brides and filling you with all kinds of wedding inspiration, right? Hence, we chalked out some mesmerizing and breathtaking minimal mehndi designs for hands for all you brides who would prefer giving ‘full bridal mehndi designs’ a miss!

    These minimal and simple mehndi designs are equally pretty and alluring for a bride to wear as a fancy adornment at their wedding.

    Image: Shashank Issar

    A Hand Full of Beaded Strings

    A beautiful flower centered pattern with tiny beads amping up the entire mehndi for hands.

    Mehndi: Henna by Pallavi

    Artsy Motifs

    Getting your hands etched with simplistic motifs in the center is a great options for brides who want to go absolute minimal on their bridal mehndi.

    Mehndi: Amrita Henna

    Mehndi: Amrita Henna

    Mehndi: The Mehndi Fairy

    Bracelet Patterns

    These mehndi designs with minimal patterns in the middle and on the fingers with a bracelet style design on the wrists looks extremely bridal while being minimalist ones.

    Mehndi: Bharathi Sanghani Mehndi

    Mehndi: Henna by Divya

    Mehndi: Minal Beauty

    Mehndi: Anjali Henna

    Mehndi: Bharathi Sanghani Mehndi

    An all Floral Affair

    This floral mehndi design with small leafs all over and dotted strings is a remarkably charming design.

    Mehndi: Henna by Mahira Mohsin

    Minimal Front Hand Mehndi Designs

    Done in flowy bels, geometric and moroccan patterns, check designs and florals, these minimally done mehndi designs for hands should definitely make it to your screenshots!

    Mehndi: Henna Lounge

    Image: Henna by Mahira Mohsin

    Mehndi: Jesma Mithun

    Mehndi: Mehndi by Hayat

    Mehndi: Henna by Mahira Mohsin

    Mehndi: Minal Beauty

    Mehndi: Minal Beauty

    Back Hand Mehndi Designs

    Let your hands breathe in the surreal intricacy and art of such light mehndi designs for your back hands.

    Mehndi: Henna by Mahira Mohsin

    Mehndi: Amrita Henna

    Mehndi: Henna Paradise

    Mehndi: Jesma Mithun

    Mehndi: Henna by Mahira Mohsin

    Mehndi: Minal Beauty

    Shorter Designs for Front Hands

    Mehndi designs ending right around the wrist and done with empty spaces in between are a fab alternative for brides who want to ditch full hand mehndi designs yet, don't want to go all minimal.
